Veneers & Crowns

Our Chandler, 3D dental practice offers full-coverage dental crowns, made from luminous porcelain and Zirconia materials. Dental crown restorations offer beautiful aesthetic results that protect your natural tooth from further damage or decay. They're also perfect for topping off your dental implants. Dr. Lopez is certified to use the CEREC crown restoration system. This same day dental crown system makes it possible for patients to receive a customized crown in just a few hours rather than waiting 2 weeks for a crown to be created and placed.
We strive to make our Irvine Endodontics office a comfortable environment where you will be sure to leave with your tooth pain addressed and a smile on your face
Same Day Crowns
CEREC same day dental crowns are extremely durable, with studies showing that Cad Cam ceramics are the 2nd longest lasting restoration behind gold. Dr. Lopez often recommends same day crowns because of their benefits, such as:
  • Less tooth sensitivity
  • Ability to prepare the tooth
  • Highly accurate digital impressions
  • Greater convenience toward the patient's schedule
  • No room for human error
