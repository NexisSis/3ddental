How do they work?
Dermal fillers have a 'plumping' effect when injected into the skin. Put simply dermal fillers will plump-up the area which has received the injection, levelling it with other parts of the skin. Ultimately, different fillersmay achieve this goal in slightly different ways, but the result is that the skin will look plump, firm, young and refreshed.
