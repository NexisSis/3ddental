Dr. Angela McMullin
Is our most recent member of the team. Her experience includes two years as the lead general dentist at a corporate practice in South East Florida. Prior to her corporate practice experience, Dr. McMullin spent two years in clinical training at the Kornberg School of Dentistry in Philadelphia.
Dr. McMullin is passionate about all aspects of health care; in particular as it pertains to oral health and the impact it has on overall wellness. Dr. McMullin is an enthusiastic integrator of technology within her practice and she is an avid student; pursuing a great many passions through continuing education courses and certification programs.
